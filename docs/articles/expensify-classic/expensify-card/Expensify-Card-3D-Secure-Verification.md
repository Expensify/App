Some online purchases require extra verification through **3D Secure** (sometimes called **Verified by Visa**).

3D Secure is a Visa authentication protocol used during certain online transactions. For the Expensify Card, this verification always requires entering a one-time passcode (OTP) sent by SMS.

The verification appears directly on the merchant’s website during checkout.

---

# How 3D Secure verification works for the Expensify Card

If 3D Secure is required during checkout:

1. Enter your Expensify Card details as usual.
2. After submitting payment, a verification prompt will appear.
3. Enter the one-time passcode (OTP) sent to your phone by SMS.
4. Submit the code to complete authentication.

---

# What to do if your 3D Secure transaction is declined

If you enter the wrong code or do not complete verification in time, the transaction will be declined.

To proceed:

1. Restart the checkout process with the merchant.
2. Wait for a new SMS code.
3. Enter the new code exactly as received.

If you are not receiving the SMS code, check that your phone number is correct in Expensify. 

---

# How to update the phone number linked to your Expensify Card

Your 3D Secure one-time passcodes are sent to the phone number linked to your Expensify Card.

To update the phone number used for 3D Secure verification:

1. Click **Settings > Account** in the navigation tabs on the left on Web.
2. Click **Wallet**.
3. Locate your **Physical Card**.
4. Click **Report physical card loss / damage**.
5. Select any option from the list and click **Continue**.
6. In the **Phone number** field, enter your updated phone number.
7. Click **Continue**.
8. Click the **X** to close the replacement card window.

After updating, your new phone number will be used for future 3D Secure SMS verification codes.

**Note:** Updating the phone number does not deactivate your current Expensify Card. Your card will only be deactivated if you complete the flow and click **Send me a new card!**

Updating the phone number linked to your Expensify Card is not available on mobile.

# FAQ

## Why didn’t I receive a 3D Secure SMS code?

If you did not receive a code:

- Confirm your phone number is correct in Expensify.
- Check that your mobile device has cellular service.
- Make sure SMS messages are not blocked on your device.

If the issue continues, contact Concierge.

---

## What happens if I enter the wrong verification code?

The transaction will be declined. You’ll need to restart the purchase and enter the new SMS code sent to your phone.

---

## Can 3D Secure be disabled for the Expensify Card?

No. 3D Secure is a Visa authentication requirement and cannot be disabled.

---

## Do I approve 3D Secure transactions inside Expensify?

No. The verification happens on the merchant’s checkout page. You only enter the SMS code when prompted.
