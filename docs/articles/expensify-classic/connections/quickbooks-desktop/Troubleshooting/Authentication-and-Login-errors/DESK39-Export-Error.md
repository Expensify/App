# DESK39 Export Error in QuickBooks Desktop Web Connector

If you see the error:

DESK39: Could not post data to QuickBooks Desktop.

This means QuickBooks Web Connector was unable to post exported data from Expensify to QuickBooks Desktop.

---

## Why the DESK39 Export Error Happens in QuickBooks Desktop

The DESK39 error occurs when:

- There is a communication issue between Expensify and QuickBooks Desktop.
- QuickBooks Web Connector encounters an internal processing error.
- The QuickBooks company file is not responding correctly.
- Permissions or hosting restrictions prevent the export from completing.

Additional troubleshooting requires reviewing the QuickBooks Web Connector log file.

---

## How to Download the QuickBooks Web Connector Log File

### If QuickBooks Is Installed Locally

1. Open **QuickBooks Web Connector**.
2. Click **View logs**.
3. Save the downloaded log file to your computer.

### If QuickBooks Is Hosted on a Server

1. Contact your hosting provider.
2. Ask them to open **QuickBooks Web Connector**.
3. Have them click **View logs**.
4. Request a copy of the downloaded log file.

---

## What to Do Next

After downloading the log file:

1. Share the log file with **Concierge**.
2. Include:
   - The Workspace name.
   - The approximate time the error occurred.
   - Any recent changes made to QuickBooks Desktop or the server.

Concierge will review the log file to identify the root cause and provide next steps.

---

# FAQ

## Can I Fix DESK39 Without the Log File?

In most cases, no. The log file is required to determine why the export failed.

## Does DESK39 Mean My Data Was Lost?

No. This error indicates that the export could not be posted. Your data remains intact in Expensify and QuickBooks Desktop.
