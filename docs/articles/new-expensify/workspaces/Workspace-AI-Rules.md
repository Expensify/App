AI Rules let Workspace Admins describe flexible rules in plain language, and Concierge builds the rule for you. Use them when a rule you need isn't covered by the standard rule settings, so you can apply custom logic without configuring each field manually.

AI Rules are only available on the **Control** plan, and you must be a **Workspace Admin** to add, edit, or delete them.

---

## Who can use AI Rules

- You must be a **Workspace Admin**.
- The workspace must be on the **Control** plan.
- **Rules** must be enabled for the workspace. If you don't see the **Rules** tab, [learn how to enable Workspace Rules](/articles/new-expensify/workspaces/Workspace-Rules).

---

## How to add an AI Rule

1. In the **navigation tabs** (on the left on web, and at the bottom on mobile), click **Workspaces**.
2. Click your **workspace name**.
3. Click **Rules**.
4. Scroll to the **AI Rules** section.
5. Click **Add AI rule**.
6. In the **Describe your rule** field, type the rule you want Concierge to build.
7. Click **Save**.

## How to edit an AI Rule

1. In the **navigation tabs** (on the left on web, and at the bottom on mobile), click **Workspaces**.
2. Click your **workspace name**.
3. Click **Rules**.
4. Scroll to the **AI Rules** section.
5. Click the rule you want to change.
6. Update the text in the **Describe your rule** field.
7. Click **Save**.

---

## How to delete an AI Rule

1. In the **navigation tabs** (on the left on web, and at the bottom on mobile), click **Workspaces**.
2. Click your **workspace name**.
3. Click **Rules**.
4. Scroll to the **AI Rules** section.
5. Click the rule you want to remove.
6. Click **Delete rule**.
7. When prompted to confirm, click **Delete**.

---

# FAQ

## Who can add AI Rules?

Only Workspace Admins on the **Control** plan can add, edit, or delete AI Rules.

## What happens after I save an AI Rule?

Concierge builds the rule from your description, and the rule appears in the **AI Rules** section of the **Rules** tab.

## Why don't I see the AI Rules section?

The **AI Rules** section only appears when **Rules** are enabled for a **Control** plan workspace and you're a **Workspace Admin**. If **Rules** aren't enabled, [learn how to enable Workspace Rules](/articles/new-expensify/workspaces/Workspace-Rules).
