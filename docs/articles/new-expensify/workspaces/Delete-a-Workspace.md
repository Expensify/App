# Delete a Workspace

If you're looking to remove or close a workspace you no longer need, the Workspace owner can delete it. Deleting a workspace is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Who can delete a workspace

Only the Workspace owner can delete a workspace they own. 

To learn more about the Workspace owner role and what permissions they have, see [What is a Workspace Owner](/articles/new-expensify/billing-and-subscriptions/Learn-About-Billing-Permissions#what-is-a-workspace-owner).

## How to delete a workspace

1. In the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile), go to **Workspaces > [workspace name]**.
2. Select the three dots **(⋮)** next to the workspace you want to delete. 
3. Choose **Delete workspace**.
4. Confirm that you want to delete the Workspace.

## What happens after you delete a workspace

After you confirm the deletion:

- The Workspace is permanently deleted and can't be recovered.
- Workspace rooms are archived. Historical chats remain available to view, but no new messages can be sent.
- Submitted expenses and reports remain available to workspace admins, auditors, and the original submitter.
- Unsubmitted expenses and reports remain available to the submitter and can be assigned to another workspace.
- Outstanding expenses and reports can be retracted by the submitter and assigned to another workspace.

# FAQ

## Why can't I delete my Workspace?

You must be the workspace owner to delete a Workspace. If your Workspace is on an Annual subscription, you can't delete it until the end of the current subscription term.

To learn more about the Annual subscription commitment, see [When You Can Change Your Subscription](/articles/new-expensify/billing-and-subscriptions/manage-your-subscription-and-billing/manage-subscription/Learn-About-Plan-and-Subscription-Management#when-you-can-change-your-subscription).

## What happens to my expenses after I delete a Workspace?

Submitted expenses and reports remain available to workspace admins, auditors, and the original submitter. Unsubmitted expenses and reports remain available to the submitter and can be reassigned to another Workspace.
