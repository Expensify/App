# ONL442 Export Error in QuickBooks Online

If you see the error:

ONL442: Partial export — QuickBooks Online returned an error. Please delete these transactions in QuickBooks and re-export.

This means the export was only partially completed in QuickBooks Online.

---

## Why the ONL442 Export Error Happens in QuickBooks Online

The ONL442 error is typically caused by:

- A temporary outage on the QuickBooks Online side.
- Scheduled maintenance in QuickBooks Online.
- A system interruption during the export process.

In these cases, some transactions may have been created in QuickBooks Online, while others failed.

---

## How to Check QuickBooks Online System Status

Before retrying the export:

1. Visit the QuickBooks Online status page: https://status.quickbooks.intuit.com/
2. Confirm there are no active outages or maintenance events.
3. Wait until the issue is resolved if a disruption is reported.

Once QuickBooks Online is fully operational, proceed with cleanup.

---

## How to Remove Partial Exports in QuickBooks Online

1. In QuickBooks Online, locate the transactions created from the failed export.
2. Delete any partially created bills, checks, or journal entries related to the report.
3. Confirm all partial transactions are removed.

This prevents duplicate entries when you re-export.

---

## How to Re-Export the Report in Expensify

1. Open the report in Expensify.
2. Click **Export to QuickBooks Online**.
3. Confirm the export.

The report should export successfully once QuickBooks Online is fully operational and partial transactions have been removed.

---

# FAQ

## Does ONL442 Mean My QuickBooks Online Connection Is Broken?

No. This error is usually temporary and caused by an outage or interruption on the QuickBooks Online side.

## Do I Need to Disconnect and Reconnect QuickBooks Online?

No. In most cases, you only need to:

- Wait for QuickBooks Online to resolve the outage.
- Delete any partial transactions.
- Retry the export in Expensify.
