# How to troubleshoot common QuickBooks Online errors

When your QuickBooks Online integration encounters an issue, Expensify displays an error code in your report comments or in a notification to the Preferred Exporter. Use this page to find your error code and follow the link to the detailed troubleshooting steps.

---

## How to resolve QuickBooks Online export errors

- **ONL022**: Mark the correct account as billable and sync your Workspace. [Learn how to fix the ONL022 error](Export-Errors/ONL022-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL038**: Export reimbursable expenses as a Vendor Bill instead of a Journal Entry. [Learn how to fix the ONL038 error](Export-Errors/ONL038-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL056**: Sync your Workspace and categorize all expenses to a valid QuickBooks Online account. [Learn how to fix the ONL056 error](Export-Errors/ONL056-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL077**: Disable duplicate document number warnings and sync your Workspace. [Learn how to fix the ONL077 error](Export-Errors/ONL077-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL088**: Resolve when a report creator already exists as an employee and a vendor record cannot be created. [Learn how to fix the ONL088 error](Export-Errors/ONL088-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL097**: Resolve when exporting reimbursable expenses as Journal Entries to Accounts Payable while employee records are in use. [Learn how to fix the ONL097 error](Export-Errors/ONL097-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL099**: Enable sales information for billable Items and sync your Workspace. [Learn how to fix the ONL099 error](Export-Errors/ONL099-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL118**: Associate a customer or project with all billable expenses and sync your Workspace. [Learn how to fix the ONL118 error](Export-Errors/ONL118-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL297**: Sync your Workspace and update invalid customer, project, or location tags. [Learn how to fix the ONL297 error](Export-Errors/ONL297-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL298**: Sync your Workspace and update invalid location tags. [Learn how to fix the ONL298 error](Export-Errors/ONL298-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL329**: Resolve when receipt images exceed file size limits. [Learn how to fix the ONL329 error](Export-Errors/ONL329-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL363**: Resolve when account and vendor currencies do not match during export. [Learn how to fix the ONL363 error](Export-Errors/ONL363-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL417**: Resolve when Accounts Receivable and Accounts Payable accounts are set to different currencies. [Learn how to fix the ONL417 error](Export-Errors/ONL417-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL442**: Fix a partial export that occurs due to a system outage or maintenance. [Learn how to fix the ONL442 error](Export-Errors/ONL442-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL531**: Resolve when an account, customer, vendor, item, or class has been deleted or made inactive. [Learn how to fix the ONL531 error](Export-Errors/ONL531-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL562**: Resolve when vendor and Accounts Payable currencies do not match. [Learn how to fix the ONL562 error](Export-Errors/ONL562-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL580**: Resolve when the report currency does not match the export account currency. [Learn how to fix the ONL580 error](Export-Errors/ONL580-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL583**: Resolve when a vendor already exists but cannot be matched to the report submitter. [Learn how to fix the ONL583 error](Export-Errors/ONL583-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL691**: Resolve when exporting checks with a negative or $0.00 total amount. [Learn how to fix the ONL691 error](Export-Errors/ONL691-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL715**: Resolve when an account, customer, vendor, item, or class is inactive or deleted. [Learn how to fix the ONL715 error](Export-Errors/ONL715-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL793**: Resolve when billable expenses are not coded to an appropriate account type. [Learn how to fix the ONL793 error](Export-Errors/ONL793-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL888**: Resolve when exporting to a closed reporting period. [Learn how to fix the ONL888 error](Export-Errors/ONL888-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL912**: Resolve when an account, category, or related record is inactive or deleted. [Learn how to fix the ONL912 error](Export-Errors/ONL912-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL913**: Resolve when a category or export account uses an invalid account type. [Learn how to fix the ONL913 error](Export-Errors/ONL913-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL939**: Change the export type from Journal Entry to Vendor Bill. [Learn how to fix the ONL939 error](Export-Errors/ONL939-Export-Error.md).
- **ONL945**: Resolve when a vendor cannot be found due to duplicate or conflicting email records. [Learn how to fix the ONL945 error](Export-Errors/ONL945-Export-Error.md).

# FAQ

## Where do I find my QuickBooks Online error code?

When an export or sync fails, the error code appears in the report comments and in an email notification sent to the Preferred Exporter configured in your Workspace's **Accounting** settings.

## What should I do if my error code is not listed here?

If your error code does not appear on this page, try syncing your Workspace with QuickBooks Online first. If the issue persists, contact Concierge for assistance and include the full error message.
