# Find Rooms in a Workspace

Use the Rooms directory to find and access rooms in your workspace. Workspace members can view rooms they've been added to, while workspace admins can view all rooms.

## Who can use the Rooms directory

All workspace members and workspace admins can access the Rooms directory.

The rooms you see depend on your permissions and the room's visibility settings. Workspace admins can see all rooms in the workspace. Workspace members can only see rooms they have been added to.

## How to find rooms in the Rooms directory

1. In the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile), click **Workspaces > [Workspace name]**
2. Click **Rooms**.
3. Select a room to open its details page.
4. Click **Go to room** to open the conversation.

The Rooms directory displays the rooms you can access in that workspace.

## What happens after you open a room from the Rooms directory

Selecting a room opens its details page. Workspace members can click **Go to room** to open the conversation. Workspace admins can view the room details page and manage room settings and membership.

Opening a room from the Rooms directory does not automatically join you to the room.

# FAQ

## Why can't I see a room in the Rooms directory?

Workspace members can only see rooms they have been added to. If you can't see a room, ask a workspace admin to confirm that you've been added to it.

## Does opening a room from the Rooms directory join me to the room?

No. Opening a room from the Rooms directory does not automatically join you to the room.

## Why does the Rooms directory look different for other members?

The Rooms directory only shows rooms a member is allowed to access, based on their permissions and the room's visibility settings.
