# Connect with your ExpensifyApproved! partner team in New Expensify

As an **ExpensifyApproved! Accountant**, you get a dedicated partner team focused on helping your firm grow, onboard clients faster, and get expert support whenever you need it. Your **Partner Manager**, **Account Executive**, and **Account Manager** appear on the **Help** page in New Expensify, so you can open a direct message with any of them in one click.

This guide explains where to find your partner team and what each contact helps with.

---

## Who can see the Approved! partner team on the Help page

The **Meet your Approved! partner team** section appears only for accounts enrolled as **ExpensifyApproved! Accountants**.

Each contact appears only after it has been assigned to your account:

- **Partner Manager**
- **Account Executive**
- **Account Manager**

If a contact has not been assigned yet, that contact will not appear on the **Help** page. If you are not an **ExpensifyApproved! Accountant**, you'll see the standard **Help and support** page instead.

To learn how firms join the program and earn a dedicated partner team, see [Expensify Partner Support](/articles/expensify-classic/expensify-partner-program/Your-Expensify-Partner-Manager).

---

## How to connect with your Approved! partner team

1. Click the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile).
2. Select **Settings**.
3. Select **Help**.
4. Under **Meet your Approved! partner team**, find your **Partner Manager**, **Account Executive**, or **Account Manager**.
5. Click a contact to open a direct message with them.

## What each Approved! partner team contact helps with

- **Partner Manager** — Maximize your partnership and drive referrals.
- **Account Executive** — Get your clients set up successfully.
- **Account Manager** — Ask questions and get client support.

# FAQ

## Why don't I see the Approved! partner team on my Help page?

The **Meet your Approved! partner team** section appears only if:

- Your account is enrolled as an **ExpensifyApproved! Accountant**.
- A Partner Manager, Account Executive, or Account Manager has been assigned to your account.

If neither applies, the section will not appear and you'll see the standard **Help and support** page.

## How do I message a contact on the Help page?

Click any contact under **Meet your Approved! partner team** to open a direct message with that person in New Expensify.

## Where do I get support if I don't have an assigned partner team?

Select **Concierge** on the **Help** page to chat with your personal AI agent, or open the **Help site** under **More resources** for articles and tutorials. Learn more about [what Concierge can do](/articles/new-expensify/concierge-ai/What-Concierge-Can-Do).
