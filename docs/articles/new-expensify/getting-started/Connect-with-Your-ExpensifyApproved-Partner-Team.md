# Connect with your ExpensifyApproved! partner team in New Expensify

As an **ExpensifyApproved! Accountant**, you can view and message your assigned **Partner Manager**, **Account Executive**, or **Account Manager** from the **Help** page in New Expensify.

---

## Who can connect with their Approved! partner team

The **Meet your Approved! partner team** section is available to **ExpensifyApproved! Accountants**.

The following contacts appear after they have been assigned to your account:
 - **Partner Manager**
 - **Account Executive** or **Account Manager**

To learn how firms join the program and receive a dedicated partner team, see the [ExpensifyApproved! accountants program](https://use.expensify.com/accountants-program).

---

## How to connect with your Approved! partner team

1. Click the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile).
2. Select **Account**.
3. Select **Help**.
4. Under **Meet your Approved! partner team**, find your **Partner Manager**, **Account Executive**, or **Account Manager**.
5. Click a contact to open a direct message with them.

---

# FAQ

## Why don't I see the Approved! partner team on my Help page?

The **Meet your Approved! partner team** section appears only if:

- Your account is enrolled as an **ExpensifyApproved! Accountant**.
- A Partner Manager, Account Executive, or Account Manager has been assigned to your account.

If neither applies, the section will not appear and you'll see the standard **Help and support** page.

## How do I message a contact on the Help page?

Click any contact under **Meet your Approved! partner team** to open a direct message with that person in New Expensify.

## Where do I get support if I don't have an assigned partner team?

Select **Concierge** on the **Help** page to chat with your personal AI agent, or open the **Help site** under **More resources** for articles and tutorials. Learn more about [what Concierge can do](/articles/new-expensify/concierge-ai/What-Concierge-Can-Do).
