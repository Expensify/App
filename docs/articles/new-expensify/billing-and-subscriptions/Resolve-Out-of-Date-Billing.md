# Resolve out-of-date billing in New Expensify

An out-of-date billing problem appears when Expensify can't charge the payment card on your subscription. This usually happens when the card is expired or invalid, or when a charge was declined for insufficient funds. You'll see a message that there's a billing problem with your subscription until it's resolved. As the billing owner, you can resolve it by updating your payment card and retrying the payment.

---

## Who can resolve out-of-date billing in New Expensify

Only the billing owner can update the payment card and retry the payment.

If you're a Workspace Admin but not the billing owner, you can take over billing for the workspace so you can resolve it yourself. To confirm who the billing owner is, go to the **Workspaces** tab (on the left on web, at the bottom on mobile) and check the **Billing owner** column for the relevant workspace.

---

## How to resolve out-of-date billing in New Expensify

To reach your subscription settings:

Web:
Click your profile icon, then go to **Account > Subscription** in the navigation tabs on the left.

Mobile:
Tap the **Account** tab at the bottom, then select **Subscription**.

Once you're in **Account > Subscription**, go to the **Payment** section:

1. Confirm the card on file has not expired and has sufficient funds.
2. Click **Change payment card** to update an expired or invalid card, or **Add payment card** if no card is on file.
3. Enter your card details and click **Save**.
4. Click **Retry payment** to charge the updated card.

## What happens after you resolve out-of-date billing in New Expensify

Once the payment is processed successfully:

- The billing problem message clears from your account.
- Your workspace keeps full access to all features.
- Your subscription returns to its normal billing schedule.

If the retry still fails, call your bank to authorize Expensify charges and remove any holds, then retry the payment or add a different payment card.

# FAQ

## Why do I see a billing problem on my subscription?

Expensify shows a billing problem when it can't charge your payment card, usually because the card is expired or invalid, or a charge was declined for insufficient funds. Update the card and retry the payment to clear it.

## What if I'm a Workspace Admin but not the billing owner?

You can take over billing for the workspace, which lets you add a payment card and retry the payment. Only the billing owner can resolve the billing problem.

## Will out-of-date billing limit my workspace?

An unresolved billing problem can limit new workspace activity until the payment goes through. Updating the card and retrying the payment restores full access.
