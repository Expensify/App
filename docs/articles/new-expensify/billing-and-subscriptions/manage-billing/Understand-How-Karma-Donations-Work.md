# Understand how Karma donations work

Karma donations let you automatically contribute a small percentage of your qualifying spending to Expensify.org, a nonprofit focused on eliminating injustice around the world.

Expensify automatically calculates your donation each month based on qualifying spending and charges it separately from your subscription.

There are two types of Karma donations:

- **Personal Karma** is based on the expenses you add to your own account. Learn more about [Personal Karma](https://use.expensify.com/personal-karma).
- **Corporate Karma** is based on approved company spending across your workspaces. Learn more about [Corporate Karma](https://use.expensify.com/corporate-karma).

---

## What Personal Karma and Corporate Karma are

Personal Karma and Corporate Karma are two independent donation programs that support Expensify.org using different types of spending.

**Personal Karma** is based on the expenses you create during the month, including reported, unreported, and invoice expenses.

**Corporate Karma** is based on approved expense reports, including invoices, across your company's workspaces.

Although both support Expensify.org, Personal Karma and Corporate Karma are calculated independently.

---

## How Karma donations are calculated

Karma donations are calculated from your qualifying monthly spending.

For every $500 in qualifying spending, Expensify donates $1 to Expensify.org.

For example:

* $500 in spending generates a $1 donation.
* $2,500 in spending generates a $5 donation.

Donation totals are calculated at the end of each month.

---

## How Expensify charges Karma donations

After monthly donation totals are calculated, Expensify charges the payment card associated with your Karma donation.

Karma donations are charged separately from your subscription, and the donation amount varies each month based on your qualifying spending.

After a successful charge, Expensify emails a donation receipt for your records.

---

## Which Expensify.org fund receives your donation

Every Karma donation is automatically directed to one of Expensify.org's charitable funds.

The destination fund is selected automatically using the Merchant Category Code (MCC) associated with each qualifying expense. You can't manually choose which fund receives your donation.

To learn more about the available funds, visit Expensify.org.

---

# FAQ

## Does Personal Karma include unreported expenses?

Yes. Personal Karma includes reported, unreported, and invoice expenses when calculating your monthly donation.

## Does Corporate Karma include unapproved expense reports?

No. Corporate Karma is calculated only from approved expense reports, including approved invoices, across your company's workspaces.

## Can I choose which fund receives my donation?

No. Expensify automatically selects the appropriate Expensify.org fund based on the Merchant Category Code (MCC) associated with each qualifying expense.

## Do Karma donations affect my subscription pricing?

No. Karma donations are charged separately from your subscription and don't affect your subscription pricing.
