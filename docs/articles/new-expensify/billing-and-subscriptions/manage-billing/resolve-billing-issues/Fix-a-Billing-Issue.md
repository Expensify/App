# Fix a billing issue

If Expensify can't process your subscription payment, you'll see a billing banner on the **Account > Subscription** page. The banner explains what's preventing payment and provides the action needed to resolve it.

Only the billing owner can resolve subscription billing problems.

---

## Who can fix a subscription billing problem in New Expensify

Only the billing owner can retry payments, update payment cards, authenticate payment methods, or pay outstanding subscription balances.

Subscription management is available on the web only.

---

## How to fix a subscription billing problem in New Expensify

1. Click **Account > Subscription**.
2. Review the billing banner.
3. Complete the action shown in the banner.

| If the billing banner says...                       | Do this                                                                                            |
| --------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Your card couldn't be charged                       | Click **Retry payment**. If the charge still fails, contact your bank or update your payment card. |
| Your payment card expired                           | Click **Add payment card** or update the existing payment card.                                    |
| Your payment card is expiring soon                  | Update your payment card before it expires.                                                        |
| Your payment requires authentication                | Click **Authenticate payment** and complete the verification process.                              |
| Your payment information is outdated                | Add or update your payment card to pay the outstanding balance.                                    |
| Your payment is past due                            | Pay the outstanding invoice before the due date.                                                   |
| Your account is locked because of a billing dispute | Resolve the dispute with your bank before retrying payment.                                        |

## What happens after you fix a subscription billing problem in New Expensify

After the required action is completed successfully:

* The billing banner is removed.
* Any outstanding balance is collected, if applicable.
* Your subscription returns to good standing.
* Full access to your workspace is maintained.

# FAQ

## Why do I have a subscription billing problem?

Subscription billing problems usually occur because a payment couldn't be processed, your payment card needs attention, or additional verification is required.

## Why can't I resolve the billing problem?

Only the billing owner can manage subscription billing. If you don't see the payment options, confirm you're signed in as the billing owner.

## Can I fix a subscription billing problem from the mobile app?

No. Subscription management is available on the web only.
