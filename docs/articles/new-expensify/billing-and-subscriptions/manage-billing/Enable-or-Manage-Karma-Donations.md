# Enable or manage Karma donations

You can turn Personal Karma on or off for your own account or manage Corporate Karma for your company's workspaces.

Personal Karma and Corporate Karma are managed in different places because they apply to different types of spending.

---

## Who can manage Karma donations

Any member can turn Personal Karma on or off for their own account.

Only the billing owner can turn Corporate Karma on or off because Corporate Karma donations are charged to the workspace payment card.

A valid payment card is required before either type of Karma can be enabled because donations are charged to the payment card on file.

---

## Enable or manage Personal Karma

Personal Karma is managed from the **Save the World** page in your account settings.

1. Open the **Save the World** page.
2. Turn Personal Karma on or off.
3. Add a payment card if prompted.
4. Save your changes.

Once enabled, Personal Karma automatically calculates monthly donations based on your qualifying personal expenses.

---

## Enable or manage Corporate Karma

Corporate Karma is managed from your workspace subscription settings in Expensify Classic.

1. Open your workspace subscription settings.
2. Turn Corporate Karma on or off.
3. Save your changes.

Only the billing owner can manage Corporate Karma settings.

---

## What happens after you enable Karma donations

After you enable Personal Karma or Corporate Karma, Expensify automatically calculates monthly donations based on qualifying spending.

Karma donations are charged separately from your subscription, and you receive a donation receipt after each successful charge.
