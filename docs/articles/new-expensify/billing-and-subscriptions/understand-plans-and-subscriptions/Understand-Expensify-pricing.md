# Understand Expensify pricing

Your Expensify subscription cost depends on your Workspace plan, subscription type, billable members, and, for Control Workspaces, your organization's Expensify Card usage.

## How much does each Workspace plan cost?

| Workspace plan |                        Starting price |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------: |
| **Collect**    |                   $5 per member/month |
| **Control**    | Starts at $9 per active member/month* |

Collect is always billed at a flat monthly rate per member.

Control pricing varies depending on your subscription type and your organization's qualifying Expensify Card usage.

## How are billable members counted?

The way members are billed depends on your Workspace plan.

| Workspace plan | Billable members                              |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Collect**    | Every unique member in the Workspace          |
| **Control**    | Every active member during the billing period |

An active member is someone who performs billable activity during the billing period, such as creating, submitting, approving, reimbursing, or exporting expense reports.

## What affects my subscription price?

Your subscription price may be affected by:

* Your Workspace plan
* The number of billable members
* Whether you have an annual or pay-per-use subscription
* Your organization's qualifying Expensify Card usage (Control only)

# FAQ

## Why is Control pricing shown as "starting at" $9 per member?

The lowest Control rate is available to organizations that meet the eligibility requirements for annual subscriptions and qualifying Expensify Card usage. Organizations that do not meet those requirements may pay a higher rate.

## Does Collect pricing ever change?

No. Collect is always billed at a flat monthly rate per member.

## Are taxes included in subscription pricing?

No. Applicable taxes are calculated separately based on your billing location and tax status.

## Are prices different outside the United States?

Yes. Expensify offers localized pricing in supported regions. See **Understand localized pricing** for regional pricing information.
