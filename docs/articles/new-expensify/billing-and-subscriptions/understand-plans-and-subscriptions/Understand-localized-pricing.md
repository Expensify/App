# Understand localized pricing

Expensify offers localized subscription pricing in select countries and regions. Rather than converting U.S. pricing using exchange rates, eligible customers are billed fixed prices in their local currency.

## Which currencies support localized pricing?

Localized pricing is currently available in:

| Region         | Currency  |
| -------------- | --------- |
| Eurozone       | EUR (€)   |
| United Kingdom | GBP (£)   |
| Australia      | AUD (A$)  |
| New Zealand    | NZD (NZ$) |

Customers outside these regions are billed using the default U.S. pricing.

## How localized pricing works

Localized pricing uses fixed regional rates rather than daily currency conversions. This means your subscription price remains consistent regardless of exchange rate fluctuations.

Your billing currency is determined by the payment currency set on your subscription.

## What are the localized prices?

The following table shows the standard localized pricing for each supported region.

| Region         | Collect | Control (starting at) |
| -------------- | ------- | --------------------- |
| United States  | $5 USD  | $9 USD*               |
| Eurozone       | €X      | €Y*                   |
| United Kingdom | £X      | £Y*                   |
| Australia      | A$X     | A$Y*                  |
| New Zealand    | NZ$X    | NZ$Y*                 |

*Control pricing depends on your subscription type and qualifying Expensify Card usage.

# FAQ

## How can I change my payment currency?

You can change your payment currency: 
1. In the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile), choose **Account > Subscription**.
2. Under **Payment**, select the three dots **(⋮)**.
3. Choose **Change payment currency**.

## Are localized prices converted from U.S. dollars?

No. Localized prices are fixed regional prices and are not based on daily exchange rates.

## Does localized pricing affect my subscription features?

No. Localized pricing only changes the currency and regional subscription price. The features included with your Workspace plan remain the same.

## Does Expensify Card pricing work with localized pricing?

Yes. If you're on the Control plan, any pricing adjustments based on your subscription type or qualifying Expensify Card usage still apply to your regional pricing.
