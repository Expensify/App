# How your plan and subscription determine your bill

Every paid Expensify workspace has both a **plan** and a **subscription type**. Together, they determine how your workspace is billed.

Your **plan** determines the features available to your workspace and the billing model it uses. Your **subscription type** determines how you pay for that plan. Understanding how these two settings work together makes it easier to understand your invoice.

---

## Who is affected by your plan and subscription

Your workspace's plan and subscription type apply to the entire workspace because they determine how the workspace is billed.

Only the **billing owner** can view or manage these settings.

---

## Where to find your plan and subscription

Your current plan and subscription type are shown under **Account > Subscription**.

---

## How plans and subscription types differ

Although they both affect billing, your plan and subscription type serve different purposes.

| Plan                                                 | Subscription type                              |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Determines the features available to your workspace  | Determines how you pay for your plan           |
| Options: **Collect** or **Control**                  | Options: **Annual** or **Pay-per-use**         |
| Determines the billing model used for your workspace | Determines your billing commitment and cadence |

---

## How your plan and subscription work together

Your bill is determined by combining your plan with your subscription type.

For example, a workspace on the **Collect** plan can use either an **Annual** or **Pay-per-use** subscription. The same is true for the **Control** plan.

Because plans and subscription types are independent, changing either one changes how your workspace is billed going forward.

The way billable members are counted depends on your plan. To learn how **active members** and **unique members** are defined, see [Understand Billing Terms and Definitions](../Understand-Billing-Terms-and-Definitions.md).
