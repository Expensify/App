# Fix a failed billing payment

When a subscription payment fails, Expensify shows a billing problem on your account until the charge goes through. A payment can fail when your payment card is expired, declined, or has insufficient funds. As the billing owner, you can fix this by retrying the payment or by adding a new payment card so your workspace keeps full access.

---

## Who can fix a failed billing payment

Only the billing owner can fix a failed billing payment. This includes retrying the payment and adding or changing the payment card on file.

## How to fix a failed billing payment

1. In the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile), choose **Account**.
2. Select **Subscription**.
2. Click **Retry payment** if the payment failed due to insufficient funds or a temporary decline.
3. If the card is expired or invalid, click **Change payment card** to update your card details, or **Add payment card** if no card is on file.
4. Enter your card details and click **Save**.

## What happens after you fix a failed billing payment

Once the payment is processed successfully:

- The billing problem notification on your account clears.
- Your workspace keeps full access to all features.
- Your next payment date stays on its normal schedule.

If the retry still fails, call your bank to authorize Expensify charges and remove any holds, then try again or add a different payment card.

# FAQ

## Why did my Expensify subscription payment fail?

A payment usually fails because the payment card on file is expired, was declined by your bank, or has insufficient funds. Update the card or contact your bank, then retry the payment.

## Can a Workspace Admin fix a failed payment if they aren't the billing owner?

No. Only the billing owner can retry the payment or change the payment card. A Workspace Admin would first need to take over billing for the workspace.

## Where do I see that a payment failed?

A billing problem appears on your account, and the **Payment** section under **Account > Subscription** shows the failed charge along with the **Retry payment** option.
