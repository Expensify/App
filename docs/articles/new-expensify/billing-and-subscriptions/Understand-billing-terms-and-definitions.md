# Understand billing terms and definitions

Subscription billing uses several terms to describe your workspace, subscription, and billing settings. Understanding these definitions can help you interpret billing information and manage your subscription more confidently.

Most of these terms are used by the **billing owner**, since only the billing owner manages a workspace's subscription.

---

## Subscription terminology

### Subscription

A **subscription** is the billing arrangement for a workspace. It determines how Expensify charges for your workspace and is managed from **Account > Subscription**.

### Plan

A **plan** determines which features are available for your workspace.

Expensify offers two plans:

* **Collect**
* **Control**

### Subscription type

A **subscription type** determines how you pay for your plan.

Subscription types include:

* **Annual** — a 12-month commitment with discounted pricing.
* **Pay-per-use** — month-to-month billing with no annual commitment.

A plan and a subscription type are separate. For example, a Control workspace can use either an Annual or Pay-per-use subscription.

---

## Member terminology

### Member

A **member** is anyone who belongs to a workspace.

### Active member

An **active member** performs billable activity during a billing period, such as creating, editing, submitting, approving, reimbursing, or exporting expenses.

Control plans bill based on active members.

### Unique member

A **unique member** is every member added to a workspace, whether or not they use Expensify during the billing period.

Collect plans bill based on unique members.

### Workspace Admin

A **Workspace Admin** manages workspace settings and configuration.

Workspace Admins do not automatically manage subscription billing.

### Billing owner

The **billing owner** is responsible for managing a workspace's subscription and payment method.

Learn more in **Understand billing ownership**.

## Subscription settings

### Subscription size (seats)

For annual subscriptions, subscription size (sometimes called **seats**) is the number of members included in your annual commitment.

### Auto-renew

Determines whether an annual subscription automatically renews when the current commitment ends.

### Auto-increase annual seats

Automatically increases your subscription size when your active member count exceeds your committed seats.

---

## Payment terminology

### Payment card

The **payment card** is the credit card Expensify charges for your subscription.

### Expensify Card

The **Expensify Card** is your company's corporate card for business spending.

Although their names are similar, the payment card and Expensify Card serve different purposes.

### Credit balance

A **credit balance** represents prepaid Expensify credits that can be applied toward future subscription charges.

### Discount code

A **discount code** applies eligible promotional discounts to your subscription.

### Billing currency

The **billing currency** is the currency used to charge your subscription.

### Tax exempt status

A **tax exempt status** removes eligible taxes from your subscription after approval.

## Commonly confused terms

| Instead of...                        | Understand...                                                                               |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Plan vs. Subscription type           | A plan determines available features. A subscription type determines how you pay.           |
| Active member vs. Unique member      | Control bills active members. Collect bills unique members.                                 |
| Subscription size vs. Active members | Subscription size is your commitment. Active members are your monthly usage.                |
| Payment card vs. Expensify Card      | The payment card pays your subscription. The Expensify Card is used for business purchases. |
