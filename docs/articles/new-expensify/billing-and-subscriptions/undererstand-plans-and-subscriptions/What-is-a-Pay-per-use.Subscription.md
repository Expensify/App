# What is a Pay-per-use subscription?

A **Pay-per-use subscription** is one of the subscription options available for paid Expensify workspaces. It provides a flexible billing model with no annual commitment or committed subscription size.

---

## What defines a Pay-per-use subscription

A Pay-per-use subscription has three defining characteristics:

* **No annual commitment**, allowing your subscription to adjust from month to month.
* **No committed subscription size**, so your bill reflects your workspace's active members for each billing period.
* **Monthly billing**, with charges based on your current usage rather than a long-term commitment.

How billable members are counted depends on your workspace's plan. To learn more, see **Understand billing definitions**.

---

## Why organizations choose a Pay-per-use subscription

Pay-per-use subscriptions are often used by organizations whose billable member count changes over time or that prefer the flexibility of avoiding a long-term commitment.
