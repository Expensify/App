# What is an Annual subscription?

An **Annual subscription** is one of the subscription options available for paid Expensify workspaces. It includes a 12-month commitment to a **subscription size**, providing a predictable billing model and discounted rate for organizations with consistent usage.

---

## What defines an Annual subscription

An Annual subscription has three defining characteristics:

* A **12-month commitment** that remains in effect for the duration of the subscription.
* A **subscription size**, which represents the number of billable members included in the subscription.
* **Renewal settings** that determine what happens when the subscription reaches the end of its term.

If your subscription size increases during the term, a new 12-month subscription begins at the new subscription size.

---

## Why organizations choose an Annual subscription

Annual subscriptions are often used by organizations with relatively stable usage that prefer the predictability of a long-term billing commitment, along with a discounted rate compared to Pay-per-use pricing.
