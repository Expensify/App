# What is an Annual subscription?

An **Annual subscription** is one of the subscription options available for paid Expensify workspaces. It provides a discounted billing model in exchange for a 12-month commitment.

---

## How an Annual subscription works

An Annual subscription is based on a 12-month commitment rather than month-to-month billing.

Each Annual subscription has a subscription size, which represents the maximum number of billable members included in your subscription each month.

If your organization grows beyond its committed subscription size, you can increase the subscription size. Each increase starts a new 12-month subscription at the new subscription size.

Annual subscriptions also include renewal settings that determine what happens when the subscription reaches the end of its term.

---

## How an Annual subscription differs from Pay-per-use

Both Annual and Pay-per-use subscriptions provide access to the same workspace features. The difference is how the workspace is billed.

 - Annual subscriptions include a 12-month commitment to a subscription size and are billed monthly.
 - Pay-per-use subscriptions are billed monthly with no annual commitment or committed subscription size.

The subscription you choose doesn't affect your workspace's features. Those are determined by your workspace's plan.
