# The Control plan

The **Control** plan is Expensify's advanced expense management plan. It's designed for organizations that need greater visibility, automation, and administrative control over company spending.

In addition to core expense management features, Control includes advanced approval workflows, company card management, enterprise integrations, and security controls.

---

## Who can use the Control plan

Any workspace can use the Control plan.

Only the **billing owner** can change a workspace's plan.

---

## What the Control plan includes

The Control plan includes advanced capabilities for managing expenses across larger teams and organizations, including:

* Multi-step expense approval workflows
* Company card feeds and reconciliation
* Advanced accounting integrations, including NetSuite, Sage Intacct, and QuickBooks Desktop
* HR and payroll integrations, including Gusto, TriNet, Certinia, and Workday
* SAML/SSO and other enterprise security controls

These features help organizations automate expense management while maintaining greater financial oversight.
