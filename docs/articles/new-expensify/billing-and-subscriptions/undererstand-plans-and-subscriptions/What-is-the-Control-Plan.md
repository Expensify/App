# What is the Control plan?

The **Control** plan is designed for organizations that need greater visibility and control over company spending.

It builds on Expensify's core expense management capabilities with advanced approval workflows, company card management, enterprise integrations, and additional security controls.

---

## Who is the Control plan designed for?

The Control plan is intended for growing and enterprise organizations that need to manage expenses across multiple teams, departments, or business units.

Organizations using Control can create more sophisticated approval workflows, manage company cards, connect enterprise systems, and apply additional administrative controls.

---

## What does the Control plan include?

The Control plan includes advanced expense management capabilities such as:

* Multi-step expense approval workflows
* Company card feeds and reconciliation
* Advanced accounting integrations, including NetSuite, Sage Intacct, and QuickBooks Desktop
* HR and payroll integrations, including Gusto, TriNet, Certinia, and Workday
* SAML/SSO and other enterprise security controls

Control uses the **active member** billing model. To learn how active members are defined, see **Understand billing definitions**.
