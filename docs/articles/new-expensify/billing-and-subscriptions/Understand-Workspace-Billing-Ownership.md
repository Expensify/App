# Understand Workspace Billing Ownership

Every paid workspace has an Owner. The Oener is responsible for paying the workspace's subscription and is the only person who can manage billing-related settings like plans and sunscriptions. 

Billing ownership is separate from workspace administration. Understanding the difference helps explain why some members can manage a workspace but cannot change its subscription.

---

## What is a billing owner?

The billing owner is the person whose payment card is charged for the workspace's subscription.

Each workspace has exactly one billing owner.

Only the billing owner can:

* Add or change the payment card
* Retry failed subscription payments
* Manage subscription settings
* Cancel the subscription
* Delete a paid workspace

The billing owner also receives subscription billing alerts and billing-related notifications.

You can confirm who is the Owner of a workspace you are member by clicking **Workspaces** the in the navigation tabs and viewing the **Owner** column for the workspace. 

---

## Billing owner vs. Workspace Admin

Although these roles often overlap, they are not the same.

| Billing owner                             | Workspace Admin                               |
| ----------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Pays for the workspace subscription       | Manages the workspace                         |
| Manages billing and subscription settings | Manages members and workspace configuration   |
| Receives billing alerts                   | Does not automatically receive billing alerts |
| One per workspace                         | Multiple admins are allowed                   |

A Workspace Admin does not automatically gain permission to manage billing.

---


## Why billing ownership matters

Because subscription billing affects payment methods, charges, and subscription commitments, only the billing owner can make billing changes.

If you cannot:

* View the Subscription page
* Update the payment card
* Retry a failed payment
* Cancel the subscription

you are probably not the billing owner.

---

## Billing ownership across multiple workspaces

Billing ownership is assigned per workspace.

If you own multiple workspaces, you may be the billing owner for some workspaces but not others. Likewise, another member may be responsible for billing on a workspace where you are an admin.

Each workspace manages its subscription independently.

---

## Transferring billing ownership

A Workspace Admin can transfer billing ownership to themselves if they need to assume responsibility for a workspace's subscription.

For instructions, see **How to transfer billing ownership**.
