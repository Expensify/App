# Validate a Business Bank Account

---

## Who can validate a business bank account

Only **Workspace Admins** can complete this step. If the account was shared with you, you must enter the test transactions to activate your copy.

---

## Where to find pending business bank accounts

You'll see a **Pending** badge next to the bank account that needs validation:

- Go to **Workspaces > [Workspace Name] > Workflows**.
- Or go to **Settings > Wallet** and look for the account with a **Pending** badge.

---

## How to validate a business bank account with test transactions

1. Within 1–2 business days, Expensify sends three test transactions to your bank account: two small withdrawals and one small deposit.
2. Log into your bank and find the three test amounts.
3. Return to Expensify and go to **Workspaces > [Workspace Name] > Workflows**, or go to **Settings > Wallet**.
4. Click the pending bank account.
5. Enter all three amounts exactly as they appear.
6. Click **Validate**.

Once the values are confirmed, your business bank account is fully connected and ready to use.

## What to do if test transactions don't arrive

If the test deposits don't appear after two business days:

1. Confirm that your bank account supports ACH direct debits.
2. Share the following ACH details with your bank:
   - **ACH Company IDs:** 1270239450, 4270239450, 2270239450
   - **ACH Originator Name:** Expensify
3. If still missing, contact Concierge to re-trigger the test transactions.

---

# FAQ

## How many test transactions will I receive?

Three in total: two small withdrawals and one small deposit. These are temporary and used to confirm your ownership of the account.

## What happens if I enter the wrong amounts?

You'll be prompted to try again. Multiple failed attempts may lock the validation process, so check your bank details carefully.

## Can I skip validation if the account was shared with me?

No. US-based admins must enter the test transactions to activate their copy of the account. For non-US accounts, validation may not be required.

## What if I don't have access to the bank account?

Another Workspace Admin with bank access should complete the validation. You won't be able to proceed without entering the test amounts.
