# How Concierge Analyzes Spend

Concierge may proactively review workspace activity to identify notable spend patterns, highlight meaningful changes, and surface useful insights. You can also ask Concierge questions about your expense data directly and get data-driven answers in the chat.

These analyses are powered by the same data and reporting engine used in **Insights**, but Concierge surfaces them in chat instead of requiring you to run reports manually.

To explore and analyze spend with visual dashboards, [learn how to use Insights](/articles/new-expensify/insights/How-to-Use-Insights-in-Expensify). 

---

## What kinds of spend analysis insights Concierge shares

You might see spend analysis messages that:

- Highlight changes in spending over time  
- Call out large or unusual purchases
- Surface trends across categories, merchants, or members
- Identify concentrated areas of spend  
- Ask questions to help clarify or validate patterns  

These spend analysis insights are generated automatically using your workspace data. The format, frequency, and content of these messages may change over time as we continue improving how Concierge analyzes and presents information.

When Concierge shares a spend analysis message, it may also suggest a few follow-up questions you can tap to explore the data further.

---

## How to ask Concierge about your expenses

You can ask Concierge questions about your expense data and it will look up the answer for you, so you don't have to build a search or stitch results together yourself.

To ask, send your question to Concierge in a supported chat. Concierge reads the context of where you're chatting to scope the answer, so you don't need to repeat details like the workspace name.

Examples:

- "Who submitted the most Travel expenses last month?"
- "Show all expenses over $500 this quarter."
- "Which categories increased the most from last month?"
- "Which expenses are still unapproved?"
- "How does March spending compare to February?"

After Concierge answers, it may suggest a few follow-up questions to help you dig deeper.

Concierge currently answers direct lookup questions about your expense data. If a question is too complex to answer this way, Concierge will let you know and point you to the **Search** page so you can explore it yourself. [Learn how to use search operators to filter and analyze spend](/articles/new-expensify/reports-and-expenses/Use-Search-Operators-to-Filter-and-Analyze).

## Where Concierge shares spend analysis insights

When Concierge analyzes spend proactively, it sends a message to the #admins room for that workspace. The message is visible to all members of that room.

Insights are shared periodically and may vary in timing, format, and level of detail.

You can ask Concierge your own expense questions in the #admins room for a workspace, or in your Concierge chat. 
 - In the #admins room, answers are scoped to that workspace.
 - In your Concierge chat, questions can span all of the workspaces you have access to.

[Learn how to find the #admins room for a workspace](/articles/new-expensify/chat/Expensify-Chat-Rooms-for-Admins).

## How Concierge spend analysis relates to Insights

Concierge and **Insights** use the same underlying data but serve different purposes:

- Concierge proactively delivers relevant insights based on notable patterns, and answers your expense questions in natural language directly in chat.
- **Insights** lets you analyze data and explore patterns on demand using visual dashboards.

If you need deeper customization, learn how to [use search operators to filter and analyze spend](/articles/new-expensify/reports-and-expenses/Use-Search-Operators-to-Filter-and-Analyze).

# FAQ

## How often will I receive Concierge spend analysis insights?

There’s no set schedule. Concierge may share insights from time to time based on what’s happening in your data.

## Can I control which Concierge spend analysis insights I receive?

Not at the moment. Concierge spend analyses are generated automatically.

## Can I customize Concierge spend analysis insights?

No. Concierge insights are automatically generated and cannot be customized. For custom analysis, use **Insights**.

## Why am I receiving Concierge messages about spending?

Concierge looks for meaningful changes or trends in your workspace—like shifts in spending, large transactions, or recurring patterns—and surfaces insights that may be helpful.

## What kinds of questions can I ask Concierge about my expenses?

You can ask direct lookup questions about your expense data, such as who spent what, spend by category, merchant, employee, or tag, comparisons across time periods, and the status of expenses or reports. If a question is too complex to answer directly, Concierge will point you to the **Search** page.

## Where can I ask Concierge about my expenses?

You can ask in the #admins room for a workspace, where answers are scoped to that workspace, or in your Concierge chat, where questions can span all of the workspaces you have access to. Concierge respects your access level, so you'll only see data you're allowed to view.
