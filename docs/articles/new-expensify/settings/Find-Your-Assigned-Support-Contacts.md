Paid Workspace Admins may have dedicated support contacts assigned to their account, including an Account Manager, Partner Manager, or Setup Specialist. These contacts appear directly on the **Help** page in Expensify so you can quickly start a conversation with them.

## Who can see assigned support contacts

You must be a Workspace Admin on a paid plan. Support contacts only appear if they have been assigned to your account.

- **Account Manager** - Your dedicated contact for day-to-day product questions and account support.
- **Partner Manager** - Your strategic partner contact, available to ExpensifyApproved! partners who have completed ExpensifyApproved! University.
- **Setup Specialist** - Your onboarding contact who helps with initial Expensify setup and configuration.

## How to find your assigned support contacts on the Help page

1. Go to **Settings** > **Help**.
2. Below **Concierge**, look for your assigned contacts. Each contact shows their name, avatar, and role (e.g., "Your account manager").
3. Click a contact to open a direct message with them.

# FAQ

## Why don't I see any support contacts on the Help page?

Support contacts only appear if you are a Workspace Admin on a paid plan and have been assigned an Account Manager, Partner Manager, or Setup Specialist. If none are assigned, these items won't appear.

## How do I get an Account Manager assigned?

Account Managers are assigned to accounts with subscriptions of 10 or more members. Contact **Concierge** if you believe your account qualifies.

## How do I get a Partner Manager assigned?

Partner Managers are assigned after you complete **ExpensifyApproved! University** training. Visit [university.expensify.com](https://university.expensify.com) to get started.
