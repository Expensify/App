# How to Change Your Expensify Workspace Plan

This article applies to both New Expensify and Expensify Classic. If you're the billing owner, you can switch your workspace between the Collect and Control plans based on your team's needs.

---

## Who Can Change the Expensify Workspace Plan

Only the billing owner can change the workspace plan.

---

## How to Change Your Workspace Plan in New Expensify

1. Use the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile) to go to **Account** > **Subscription**.
2. Click **Explore all plans**.
3. Choose the plan that fits your needs.

---

## How to Change Your Workspace Plan in Expensify Classic

**Web:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Workspaces** > **[Workspace Name]**.
2. Click **Overview** and select **Switch** under your desired plan.

**Mobile:**
1. Open the Expensify app.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Workspaces** > **[Workspace Name]** > **Current Plan** > **Switch**.

---

# FAQ

## Will I be charged for my subscription even if I don't use SmartScans?

Yes. Collect and Control subscriptions are prepaid and not usage-based, so you'll be charged regardless of activity.

## Do I need my own subscription if I'm already in a workspace?

No. Workspace members already have unlimited SmartScans. You only need a personal subscription if you leave your company's workspace and want to continue using Expensify individually.
