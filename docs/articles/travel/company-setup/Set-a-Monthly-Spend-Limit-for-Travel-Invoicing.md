# Set a monthly spend limit for Travel Invoicing

Workspace Admins can set a monthly spend limit per member on Travel Invoicing cards to control how much each member can spend on travel each month. When a member reaches the limit, they are blocked from making new travel bookings until the next month.

## Who can set a monthly spend limit for Travel Invoicing

Only **Workspace Admins** with Travel Invoicing enabled on their workspace can configure this setting.

If Travel Invoicing is not enabled, learn how to [enable Travel on a workspace](https://help.expensify.com/articles/travel/company-setup/Enable-Travel-on-a-Workspace).

## How to set a monthly spend limit per member for Travel Invoicing

1. In the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile), click **Workspaces**.
2. Click your workspace name.
3. Click **Travel**.
4. Under the Travel Invoicing section, click **Monthly spend limit per member**.
5. Enter the maximum amount each member can spend on travel per month.
6. Click **Save**.

## How to reduce the monthly spend limit for Travel Invoicing

1. Follow the steps above to open the **Monthly spend limit per member** setting.
2. Enter a lower amount than the current limit.
3. Click **Save**.
4. A confirmation modal appears warning that members who have already exceeded the new limit will be unable to make new travel bookings until next month.
5. Click **Confirm** to apply the reduced limit.

## What happens after you set a monthly spend limit for Travel Invoicing

- Each member's travel spending is tracked against the limit on a monthly basis.
- Members who reach the limit are blocked from making new travel bookings until the following month.
- If you reduce the limit below what a member has already spent, that member cannot make new bookings until next month.
- The setting updates optimistically and works offline.

# FAQ

## Can I set different spend limits for different members?

No. The monthly spend limit applies equally to all members in the workspace. Every member shares the same per-person cap.

## What happens if I set the limit to $0?

A $0 limit means no per-member spending cap is enforced. Members can book travel without a monthly restriction.
