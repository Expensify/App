# Book travel using Travel Invoicing

If your company uses Travel Invoicing, you can book travel without paying out of pocket.

Instead of using a personal or company card, your booking is charged to your Workspace’s central billing account.

This means:
- You don’t need to front money
- You don’t need to submit an expense
- Your company pays for the trip directly

---

## Who can book travel using Travel Invoicing

- Members on a Workspace with Travel enabled
- Members on a Workspace with Travel Invoicing enabled

If you’re unsure, ask your Workspace Admin.

---

## How to pay for a booking using Travel Invoicing

### Step 1: Start a travel booking

1. Click the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile).
2. Select **Travel**.
3. Click **Book travel**.

## What happens after you book travel

- Your booking is confirmed through Expensify Travel
- The payment is charged to your company’s Travel Invoicing account
- For flights and rail tickets, the expense and receipt will be added to your Expensify account. For hotel and car bookings, you will need to upload the receipt after checking-out/returning the car
- The charge appears in your company’s Travel Invoice statement

# FAQ

## Why do I sometimes need a CVV to book travel?

Travel Invoicing uses a virtual card behind the scenes. The CVV is required to complete the payment securely.

## Do I need to use my personal card?

No. Your company pays for the booking through Travel Invoicing.

## What if I don’t see Travel CVV?

You may not be on a Workspace with Travel Invoicing enabled, or you may not be travel-enabled.
