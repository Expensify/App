# Book travel using Travel Invoicing

If your company uses Travel Invoicing, you can book travel without using a personal card.

Instead of paying for travel yourself, your booking is charged directly to your company's Travel Invoicing account.

This means:
- You don't need to use a personal card to pay for the booking.
- Your company is billed directly for the trip.
- Travel expenses are tracked through Expensify Travel.

---

## Who can book travel using Travel Invoicing

- Members on a Workspace with Travel enabled.
- Members on a Workspace with Travel Invoicing enabled.

If you're unsure whether Travel Invoicing is available, contact your Workspace Admin.

Workspace Admins can [learn how to enable Travel on a Workspace](/articles/travel/company-setup/Enable-Travel-on-a-Workspace).

---

## How to book travel using Travel Invoicing

1. Click the navigation tabs (on the left on web, on the bottom on mobile).
2. Select **Travel**.
3. Click **Book travel**.
4. Search for flights, hotels, rental cars or rail tickets.
5. Select your preferred itinerary.
6. Continue to checkout.
7. In the **Payment method** section, select **Expensify Travel Card**.
8. If a CVV is requested, click **Go to wallet** in the blue banner at the top of the page.
9. Select **Travel CVV**.
10. Click **Reveal details**.
11. Copy the CVV.
12. Return to checkout and enter the CVV to complete the booking.

[Learn how to book a hotel, flight, rental car or rail ticket with Expensify travel](https://help.expensify.com/travel/hubs/booking-travel/)

---

## What happens after you book travel using Travel Invoicing

- Your booking is confirmed through Expensify Travel.
- The payment is charged to your company’s Travel Invoicing account.
- For flights and rail tickets, the expense and receipt are automatically added to your Expensify account.
- For hotel and car bookings, upload the receipt after checkout or after returning the vehicle.
- The charge appears in your company’s Travel Invoice statement

# FAQ

## Why do I need a CVV when booking travel?

Travel Invoicing uses a virtual card to pay for travel bookings. Some travel providers require a CVV to complete the transaction.

## Do I need to use my personal card to pay for travel bookings?

No. Travel bookings made with Travel Invoicing are charged directly to your company's Travel Invoicing account.

## Why don't I see Travel CVV?

You may not be travel-enabled, or your Workspace may not have Travel Invoicing enabled. Contact your Workspace Admin if you're unsure.
